# LoRe4Health
### NeurIPS 2026 Workshop on Longitudinal Reasoning Over Evolving Evidence for Healthcare

Official workshop website:  https://ai-mind-lab.github.io/lore4health

---

## About

LoRe4Health focuses on longitudinal reasoning systems capable of integrating evolving multimodal clinical evidence across patient trajectories. The workshop brings together researchers working on multimodal reasoning, retrieval-grounded inference, uncertainty-aware clinical AI, clinician supervision, deployment challenges, and trustworthy healthcare AI systems.

The workshop emphasizes reasoning over evolving evidence rather than isolated prediction tasks, with applications spanning medical imaging, pathology, genomics, electronic health records, and multimodal foundation models for healthcare.

---

## Topics

- Longitudinal clinical reasoning
- Multimodal healthcare foundation models
- Retrieval-grounded clinical inference
- Temporal reasoning over patient trajectories
- Trustworthy and uncertainty-aware healthcare AI
- Clinician-AI interaction
- Deployment and monitoring of clinical AI systems
- Benchmarking and evaluation for clinical reasoning
